About piRoot Beta 2

Beta 2 includes Ubuntu, Debian, and Windows 98.

Windows 98 doesn't come pre-installed with piRoot. Here is how to load it.

Go to winworldpc.com, and go to library. Go to Windows 98, then click Second Edition.

Download Windows 98 OEM Full. Extract, and rename the iso win98.iso . Make sure you extract it to your home directory.

After that, run sudo piroot-win. Now your done!
